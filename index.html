Who Am I?

I am currently employed as a Production Management Technology Associate at the Wells Fargo N.A. corporate offices.

I have a Computer Science BA from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities.

During my time at the University of Minnesota, my research group for the graduate course, CSCI 5271, partnered with the OpenWPM research group in Princeton University.
