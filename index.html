Who Am I?

I am currently an Production Management Technology Associate at the Wells Fargo N.A. corporate offices.

I specialize in Computer programming, infrastructure, and security.

I have a Computer Science BA from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities.

For the summer of 2016, I was an intern at Wells Fargo in downtown Minneapolis for their Technology Operations Group Intern Program in the Production Management Technologies Department.

Before that, during the school year, I was a Student Database Technician for the University of Minnesota's Disability Resource Center.
