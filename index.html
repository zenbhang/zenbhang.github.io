Who Am I?

I am a student at the University of Minnesota enrolled in the Computer Science BA program.

For the 2015-2016 academic year, I am the acting Vice-Chair of IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers).

I work on many side projects, some with friends and colleauges I met at the University of Minnesota, some I work on personally.

I have programmed several popular Rainmeter skins, most notably, a revision of the Monstercat Player as well as the self-dubbed Zen Bar, detailed below.

I am also currently curating the defunct AlfredProject, a project that I started early in high school.
